Stir-fried Noodles

Golden, flaky pastry filled with seasoned meat and veggies — perfect for snacking or lunch on the go.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 200g instant or egg noodles
  • 1 carrot (julienned)
  • 1 bell pepper (sliced)
  • ½ cup shredded cabbage
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tsp sesame oil (optional)
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • Optional: diced chicken, shrimp, or tofu

Directions

  • Cook noodles, drain, and set aside.
  • Heat oil in a wok or large pan. Add garlic and any protein (chicken/shrimp) if using. Stir-fry for 2-3minutes.
  • Add vegetables and stir-fry until tender but crisp.
  • Add noodles, soy sauce, and sesame oil. Toss everything together over high heat.
  • Serve hot.
