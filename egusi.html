Egusi Soup

A rich, earthy vegetable soup from the South-South of Nigeria, made with okazi and waterleaf.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups ground egusi (melon seeds)
  • 500g assorted meats (beef, tripe, etc.)
  • 1 cup palm oil
  • 1 medium onion (blended)
  • 3 cups spinach or ugu leaves (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp ground crayfish
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 2 cups stock or water

Directions

  • Season and boil meats until tender. Set aside.
  • Heat palm oil, add blended onion and fry for 2 mins.
  • Stir in egusi and fry for 5-7 minutes, stirring constantly.
  • Add meat stock gradually while stirring to avoid lumps.
  • Add crayfish, seasoning cubes, and meats. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Add vegetables, stir, and cook for another 5 minutes. Serve with pounded yam or eba.
