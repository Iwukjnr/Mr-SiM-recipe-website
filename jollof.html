Jollof Rice with Chicken

This protein-packed Omelette is loaded with colorful vegetables, making it a healthy and satisfying start to the day.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups long grain rice (washed)
  • 1 whole chicken (cut into parts)
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion (divided)
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 tsp thyme
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • Â½ cup vegetable oil

Directions

  • Season chicken with salt, pepper, thyme, and half an onion. Boil until cooked, then fry or grill. Set aside.
  • Blend tomatoes, peppers, garlic, and remaining onion into a smooth mix.
  • Heat oil, add tomato paste, and fry for 2 minutes. Add blended mixture and cook until it reduces.
  • Add rice, chicken stock, and seasoning cubes. Cook on low heat until rice is soft and liquid absorbed.
  • Stir in grilled chicken and steam for 5 minutes. Serve hot.
