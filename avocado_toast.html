Avocado Toast

A nutritious morning boost - creamy avocado and perfectly poached egg on crispy toast, topped with a dsh of seasoning

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread (sourdough or wholegrain)
  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp white vinegar
  • Salt and black Pepper
  • Optional: chili flakes, lemon juice

Directions

  • Toast the bread until golden
  • Mash the avocado with a pinch of Salt, pepper and a squeeze of lemon juice
  • Bring a small pot of water to a light simmer, add vinegar.
  • Crack egg into a small bowl, then gently slide it into the water. Poach for 3-4 minutes
  • Spread mashed avocado on toast, top with poached Egg.
  • Sprinkle with chili flakes or extra pepper to taste
