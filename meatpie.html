Meat pie

Golden, flaky pastry filled with seasoned meat and veggies — perfect for snacking or lunch on the go.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 300g minced meat
  • 2 medium potatoes (diced)
  • 1 carrot (diced)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 1 seasoning cube
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp flour (for thickening)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 250g margarine or butter
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Cold water (as needed)
  • 1 egg (for glazing)

Directions

  • Filling: Heat oil, sauté onions, then add meat. Cook until brown. Add carrots, potatoes, seasoning, and flour. Cook until soft and thickened. Cool.
  • Dough: Mix flour, salt, and baking powder. Rub in margarine. Add cold water bit by bit to form a dough.
  • Roll out dough, cut into circles. Fill with meat, fold over, and seal edges.
  • Glaze with beaten egg. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 25-30 minutes.
