Afang Soup

A rich, earthy vegetable soup from the South-South of Nigeria, made with okazi and waterleaf.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 500g beef and/or stockfish
  • 1 cup water leaves (or spinach)
  • 1 cup afang (okazi) leaves (blended or pounded)
  • 1 cup periwinkle (optional)
  • Â½ cup ground crayfish
  • 1 tbsp ground pepper
  • 1 cup palm oil
  • Seasoning cubes and salt

Directions

  • Cook meat and stockfish with salt and seasoning cubes until soft.
  • Add palm oil, crayfish, and pepper. Simmer for 5-7 minutes.
  • Add water leaves and cook for 3 minutes.
  • Stir in afang leaves and periwinkle (if using).
  • Cook until well combined and thick. Serve with fufu.
