Nigerian Beef Stew

Tomato-based stew simmered with fried beef and bold spices — a delicious companion to rice or yam.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 500g beef (boiled and fried)
  • 4 tomatoes
  • 2 red bell peppers
  • 1 onion (divided)
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • Salt and chili pepper

Directions

  • Blend tomatoes, peppers, and half an onion.
  • Heat oil, fry chopped onions, then add tomato paste and fry for 2 minutes.
  • Add blended mixture and cook until oil separates (10-15 mins).
  • Add fried beef, seasoning, and adjust salt/pepper. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  • Serve with rice, plantain, or yam.
