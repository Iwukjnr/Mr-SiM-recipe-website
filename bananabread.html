Banana Bread

A moist, flavorful loaf made with ripe bananas — perfect as a dessert or a sweet snack. This comforting classic is soft, rich, and full of natural sweetness.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


20 minutes

cook


40 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 3 ripe bananas (mashed)
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • ½ cup melted butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 175°C (350°F). Grease a loaf pan.
  • In a bowl, mix mashed bananas, eggs, sugar, melted butter, and vanilla.
  • In another bowl, combine flour, baking soda, and salt.
  • Combine wet and dry ingredients. Stir until just mixed.
  • Pour into pan and bake for 50-60 minutes.
  • Cool before slicing and serving.
