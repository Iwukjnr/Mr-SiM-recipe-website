prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 1½ cups all-purpose flour
  • 3½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • 1¼ cups milkm
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp melted butter (plus more for frying)

Directions

  • In a bowl, whisk together flour, baking powder, sugar, and salt.
  • In another bowl, mix milk, egg and melted butter
  • Combine wet and dry ingredients (batter should be slightly lumpy)
  • Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat and grease lightly.
  • pour ¼ cup of batter per panake. Cook until bubbles form, then flip and cook until golden brown.
  • Serve warm with syrup or friuts
