Beef Tacos

Beef Tacos are a flavorful Mexican favorite made with seasoned ground beef, fresh toppings, and soft or crispy taco shells. Perfect for quick weeknight dinners or festive gatherings, these tacos are delicious, customizable, and always a hit!

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 200g ground beef
  • Â½ onion (chopped)
  • 1 clove garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper
  • Taco shells (6)
  • Toppings: shredded lettuce, cheese, diced tomatoes, sour cream

Directions

  • In a skillet, cook onion and garlic in a bit of oil until soft.
  • Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  • Add chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  • Warm taco shells in a dry pan or oven.
  • Fill with beef and top with desired toppings.
