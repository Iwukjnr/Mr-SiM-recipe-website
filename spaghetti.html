Spaghetti Carbonara

Italian-inspired and Nigerian-loved — spaghetti tossed in a rich, meaty tomato sauce with a savory twist.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 200g spaghetti
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 100g bacon or pancetta (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt and black pepper

Directions

  • Boil spaghetti in salted water until al dente. Reserve ½ cup pasta water.
  • Cook bacon in a pan until crispy. Add garlic and cook briefly.
  • Beat eggs with Parmesan, salt, and pepper.
  • Drain pasta, then mix quickly with the bacon and garlic.
  • Remove from heat and stir in egg mixture. Add pasta water gradually to make a creamy sauce.
  • Serve immediately with extra cheese.
