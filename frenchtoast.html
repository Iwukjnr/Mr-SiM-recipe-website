French toast with syrup

Golden slices of bread soaked in spiced egg mixture and pan-fried, then drizzled with syrup — comfort food at its finest.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread (thick-cut preferred)
  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • ½ tsp cinnamon
  • Butter for frying
  • Maple syrup and powdered sugar for topping

Directions

  • In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon.
  • Dip each slice of bread into the mixture, soaking both sides.
  • Melt butter in a pan over medium heat.
  • Fry bread slices until golden on both sides.
  • Serve with syrup, fruits, or powdered sugar.
