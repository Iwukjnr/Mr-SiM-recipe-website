Puff Puff

Soft, deep-fried balls of dough with a slightly sweet taste - a beloved Nigerian street snack.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup sugar
  • 2 tsp yeast
  • 1¼ cups warm water
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp nutmeg (optional)
  • Oil (for frying)

Directions

  • Mix yeast and warm water. Let sit 5 minutes to activate.
  • In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, salt, and nutmeg. Add yeast mixture.
  • Mix into a sticky batter. Cover and let rise for 1 hour.
  • Heat oil. Scoop small balls of batter into hot oil and fry until golden brown.
  • Drain and serve warm.
