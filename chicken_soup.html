Chicken Pepper Soup

Spicy, aromatic broth with tender chicken â€” perfect for cold days or a light yet comforting meal.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


20 minutes

cook


30 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 tbsp pepper soup spice mix
  • 1 tbsp ground crayfish
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 1 onion (sliced)
  • Scotch bonnet (to taste)
  • Scent leaves or basil
  • Salt to taste

Directions

  • In a pot, combine chicken, onions, seasoning cubes, and salt. Boil for 10 minutes.
  • Add pepper soup spice, crayfish, and chopped pepper.
  • Add enough water to cover the meat. Simmer for 20-25 minutes.
  • Add scent leaves and cook for 5 more minutes.
  • Serve hot with yam or rice.
