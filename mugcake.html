Chocolate Mug Cake

Need a quick dessert fix? This rich, gooey chocolate cake comes together in minutes using just a mug and a microwave — warm, satisfying, and indulgent.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp flour
  • 4 tbsp sugar
  • 2 tbsp cocoa powder
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp milk
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • A pinch of salt

Directions

  • Mix all ingredients in a large microwave-safe mug.
  • Microwave on high for 1½ to 2 minutes.
  • Let cool slightly. Top with ice cream or whipped cream if desired.
