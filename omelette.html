Omelette with Veggies

This protein-packed Omelette is loaded with colorful vegetables, making it a healthy and satisfying start to the day.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


10 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 3 eggs
  • ¼ cup of chooped onions
  • ¼ cup of chopped tomatoes
  • ¼ cup of chopped bell peppers
  • ¼ cup of chopped spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil or butter

Directions

  • Crack eggs into a bowl, seaon with salt and pepper and beat well.
  • Heat oil/butter in a pan over medium heat.
  • Add onions, tomatoes, peppers and spinach. Saute for 2-3 minutes.
  • Pour in beaten eggs and cook gently until edges set.
  • Gently fold and cook until center is set.
  • Serve hot with toast or a side of fruit
