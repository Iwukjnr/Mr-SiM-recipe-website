Fruit Salad with Yogurt

A light and refreshing mix of seasonal fruits tossed in creamy yogurt. This colorful bowl is both healthy and delicious — a perfect finish to any meal.

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 1 apple (chopped)
  • 1 banana (sliced)
  • 1 orange (peeled and sectioned)
  • ½ cup pineapple chunks
  • ½ cup grapes (halved)
  • 1 cup plain or flavored yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Directions

  • Combine all fruits in a bowl.
  • Add yogurt and honey. Toss gently.
  • Chill before serving.
