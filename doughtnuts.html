Doughnuts

Golden and fluffy, these homemade doughnuts are lightly sweet, soft inside, and perfect with a sugar coating or a delicious glaze. A fun treat for any time of day!

Jump to recipe Watch video

prep


15 minutes

cook


15 minutes

serves


4 people

Ingredients

  • 2½ cups all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup sugar
  • 2¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • ½ cup warm milk
  • 2 tbsp butter (softened)
  • 1 egg
  • ½ tsp salt
  • Oil (for frying)
  • Optional: sugar or glaze for coating

Directions

  • Dissolve yeast in warm milk and let sit 5-10 minutes.
  • Mix flour, sugar, salt, egg, butter, and yeast mixture into a dough.
  • Knead and let rise for 1 hour.
  • Roll out and cut dough into circles. Let rest for 15 minutes.
  • Fry in hot oil until golden on both sides. Drain and coat in sugar or glaze.
